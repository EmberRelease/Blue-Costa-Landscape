About Blue Costa

Built on design vision and hands-on mastery.

Blue Costa is shaped by the combined strengths of Ana Weissman and Paulo Costa — pairing over 20 years of sales, business management, and design-minded leadership with more than 15 years of hands-on construction, hardscape, and landscape experience. The result is a company that approaches each project with both visual intention and real field capability, creating outdoor spaces that feel carefully composed, expertly built, and unmistakably Blue Costa.
